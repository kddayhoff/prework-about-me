Alkaline Trio: The Definition of Modern Punk

Who Are They?

Alkaline Trio consists of three members (I guess the name kind of gave that away)

  • Matt Skiba (guitar/vocals)
  • Dan Andriano (bass/vocals)
  • Derek Grant (drum kit)

What Have They done?

Alkaline has produced nine studio albums starting from 1998 and they have no plans of stopping any time soon. Their most recent album Is This Thing Cursed?dropped in 2018. Below you will find a full list of their discography.

  1. Goddamnit, 1998
  2. Maybe I'll Catch Fire, 2000
  3. From Here to Infirmary, 2001
  4. Good Mourning, 2003
  5. Crimson, 2005
  6. Agony and Irony, 2008

  9. Is This Thing Cursed?, 2018

What is Their History?

Alkaline Trio a Chicago based punk band. They have been influence by bands such as Social Distortion, Ramones, Misfits, Green Day, The Clash, Joy Division, and NOFX, just to name a few. They have been around since 1996, the original band formed by Matt Skiba, who is still their guitarist to this day. Their popularity has increased exponentially over time. Their album From Here to Infirmary rose above the Billboard Top 200 mark. They formed their own record label in 2009, Heart and Skull. Their 2013 album My Shame is True, topped the charts at #24.

Where Are They Now?

Alkaline Trio continues to rock to this day and show no signs of slowing down. Matt Skiba has ventured out to several side projects. The most notable is Blink-182. He joined the band in 2015, replacing the popular Tom DeLonge. He still currently records and tours with Blink-182, their latest album Nine having dropped in 2019. It displays Matt's clear influence on the band, producing a heavier punk sound, leaving their pop-punk days behind.
